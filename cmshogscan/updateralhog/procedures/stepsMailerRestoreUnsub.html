General notes, terminology and policies

  • This environment is different than the website. There is only one, shared, account that is used for logging in. Do NOT change the Email or the Password.
  • In this tutorial the Navagational Bar is referred to as the navbar.
    • Every website has a navbar. It normally appears at the top of the page or on the left side of the page. As a user clicks links in the navbar to go to different pages within the website, the navbar retains its position making it easy for the user to navigate to other pages within the website.
  • This tutorial covers restoring a member who unsubscribed from the email campaigns.

Restore an Unsubscribed Member

  1. From the navbar on the left click Audience to expand it. By default, the Contacts page is displayed with the Overview tab selected.
    1. On the Contacts page click the Unsubscribed tab
    2. Scroll down a little and click the Manage unsubscribe page button. see image below
  2. In the page that pops up scroll down to the Subscription link section
    1. Click the Copy icon to copy the link to your clipboard. The wording will change to Copied.
  3. Paste this link into the email being sent to the member who unsubscribed but wants to subscribe again to receive campaigns.
    1. In the email;
      1. tell the member to click the link
      2. Let them know that after clicking the link they see a message informing them an activation email has been sent to their email address
      3. Let them know they must open that email to complete the sign-up process as there is another step to take. Note: It may go to their junk/spam folder so have them look for it there. It will have a title of Subscriber Activation.
      4. Let them know that after clicking the link in the email a new tab will open with a Subsription confirmed message and email address has been successfully subscribed where email address is their email address.
  4. You will see their name removed from the Unsubscribed list and looking at your list of members you should see that their account is marked Active.

    5. That's it! The member has successfully resubscribed.
