Upload Pictures to the Galleries

General notes, terminology and policies

  • There are two environments in which you can work;
    • The sandbox environment is where you can test and try things out to familiarize yourself with the steps you will be going through. The sandbox environment starts with dev. Our sandbox environment is https://dev.raleighhog.com
    • The production environment is the site that all members see. This is where you make the changes that chapter members see. Our production environment is https://www.raleighhog.com
  • Please note that prior to doing anything in the production environment you can test in the sandbox environment first. This environment is used for testing and is not seen by members.
    • Login credentials are the same for both environments
    • Your webmaster will also help with any questions you may have.
  • National discourages HOG chapters from publishing any photographs of minor children, even with permission from a parent or guardian. We will adhere to this policy.

Upload Images

  1. Login to your account on raleighhog.com and there will be a smaller Navigational Bar (navbar) on top of the Chapter navbar. Click 'Add content. see image below
  2. Click 'Gallery' from the page that opens. see image below
    1. Note: Forum topic has been turned off for the website. It is not used and is out of scope for this document.
  3. The Create Gallery page opens. see image below
    1. Give an appropriate title. We use the following naming convention;
      1. MMM YYYY [ eventName1 + eventName2 + etc ] where MMM is the three letter abbreviation for the month and YYYY is the year. (For consistancy, in the Galleries if there are images for the chapter meeting the event name is Monthly mtg)
    2. Click the 'Browse' button
  4. The following pop-up appears where you will load the images for the gallery. see image below
    1. I've only used the 'UPLOAD' method. Instructions following are based on that
    2. Note 'Drag files here'. This is where the you will drop the images to be uploaded
      • Allowed file types are png, gif, jpg and jpeg
    3. 'Start upload' and 'Next' will be explained in the following steps
    4. Note: There is an 'Ask a question' button that has been removed from the next few images. Do not use this button as questions go to an Admin and will not be seen. Contact your webmaster if you have any questions or issues.
  5. After dragging and dropping images click 'Start upload'. see image below
  6. Depending on the number of images and size of each image being uploaded it will take a few minutes for all to upload. Wait until all images have uploaded. The following image shows the upload in progress.
  7. After all images have uploaded, click the 'Next' button. see image below
  8. Wait until all images have finished processing before continuing. The image below shows the 'please wait...' message
  9. After all images have been processed you will see a thumbnail image of each one. Click the 'Save' button to complete the upload
    1. Optionally fill in the 'Revision log message' prior to clicking 'Save'. A short message such as "initial upload of title images" (where title is the name of the gallery) is sufficient.
  10. The following screenshot shows the message that the Gallery has been successfully created
  11. To add images to an existing Gallery, click the Gallery to open it, then click the 'Edit' tab. Scroll to the bottom of the page, click the 'Browse' button and follow the steps starting at '4' to add additional images
  12. Follow all the steps above to create a new Gallery
