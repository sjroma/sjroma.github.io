Help for your New Role

Congratulations on your new role in the Raleigh NC Chapter of HOG. Your role gives you permissions to make changes on the Chapter website. Depending on your role, one of the following pages will help guide you with step by step instructions to successfully make updates.

There are two environments in which to work. Login credentials are the same for both environments.

The Sandbox environment, is where you should try things out to get comfortable with your new role. You can make changes, or try things out, without affecting what members see. Remember, it's best if you first start out by making changes in the Sandbox environment until you are comfortable making changes. It is much easier to correct any mistakes in this environment. Also, do not hesitate to contact your webmaster for help. The web address for the Sandbox environment is https://dev.raleighhog.com The Production environment, is the website that all chapter members see. This environment is where you make the updates/changes that everyone sees. The web address for the Production environment is https://www.raleighhog.com

Depending on your new role, click the link to use one of the following tutorials to help you to;

  • Add a Team Member - Editor
  • Place a Newsletter on the Website - Newsletter Editor
  • Place a Financial Report on the Website - Treasurer
  • Update the Event Calendar - Activities Officer
  • Create a Blog entry - Activities Officer
  • Upload Images to the Galleries Page - Photographer
  • Add Meeting Minutes - userid assigned
  • Upload Road Captain Documents - Head Road Captain
