Help for your New Role

Congratulations on your new role in the Raleigh NC Chapter. Your role gives you permissions to make changes on the Chapter website. Depending on your role, one of the following pages will help guide you with step by step instructions to successfully make updates.

There are two environments in which to work. The first, the Sandbox environment, is where you should try things out to get comfortable with your new role. Here you can make changes, or try things out, without affecting what members see. Remember, it's best if you first start out by making changes in the sandbox environment until you are comfortable making changes. It is much easier to correct any mistakes in this environment. Also, do not hesitate to contact your webmaster for help. The second, the Production environment, is the website that all chapter members see. This environment is where you make the updates/changes that everyone sees.

Depending on your new role, use one of the following guides to help you to;

  • create/add a Team Member - Editor
  • add a Newsletter - Newsletter Editor
  • update the Financial Reports page - Treasurer
  • add/update events to the Event Calendar - Activities Officer
  • add a Blog entry - Activities Officer
  • add images to the Galleries page - Photographer
  • upload Meeting Minutes - Photographer
  • add/update Road Captain documents - Head Road Captain
