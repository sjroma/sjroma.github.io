This is our website's navagational bar or, navbar for short. It is always at the top and is how you get to (navigate) the different pages on our website. Shown is what it looks like when using a desktop computer. It will look slightly different when using a tablet or smartphone but the same links are there.

What follows is a synopsis of the pages seen as you navigate our website.

  1. "About Us" - talks about our Raleigh HOG Chapter and has links to download the Chapter Charter and Chapter Handbook from HOG national Hover over the "About Us" link and see the following subpages;
    • "FAQ" - Frequently Asked Questions, answers questions most asked by new, and old, members
    • "Join the Chapter" - explains a bit about the Raleigh NC chapter of HOG and includes links to the forms needed for joining our chapter
    • "Policies" - Raleigh NC Chapter policies
    • "Chapter Officers" - pictures, names and email addresses of current Primary and Discretionary officers
      • "Former Chapter Officers" - must be logged in to see this subpage that shows the former officers since Raleigh HOGs inception in 1987
    • "HOG Member of the Year" - must be logged in to see this subpage showing each recipient of this award since its inception in 2005
    • "Memorial Wall" - must be logged in to see this subpage showing chapter members who've passed
  2. "Chapter Rides" - forms needed when submitting rides to the Activities Officer; also includes links to the forms Road Captains/Ride Leaders need at the start of a chapter ride Hover over the "Chapter Rides" link and see the following subpages;
    • "Road Captains" - pictures of current Road Captains Logged in Road Captains hovering over the "Road Captains" link will see the following subpages;
      • "RC Forms & Documents" - ride day forms
      • "Using the Smartphone App" - a how-to tutorial for Road Captains
    • "Chapter Patch Rides" - chapter patch rides with instructions and links to each individual pdf Hover over the "Chapter Patch Rides" link and see the following subpages;
      • "Rules & Guidelines" - specific guidelines to earning chapter patch ride patches
      • "Patch Submission" - guidelines to follow if you have an idea for a new patch
    • "Ride 365 Info" Hover over the "Ride 365 Info" link and see the following subpages;
      • "15 for '25 Ride Challenge"
      • "Dealer Check-in Challenge"
      • "50 Rides, One Nation"
      • "2025 H.O.G. Rally Schedule"
      • "Ride 365 Leaderboard" - previous year total mileage & current year running total mileage for each chapter member
      • "Ride 365/HOG App Videos" - links to HOG National's YouTube videos about navigating/using the Harley-Davidson app
    • "Group Riding Orientation" - a synopsis of how we ride as a group
  3. "Calendar" - chapter rides & events; login to RSVP to a ride/event
  4. "Galleries" - photos of chapter rides & events, grouped by month and year
  5. "Blog" - contains a page showing how to subscribe to the calendar with your smartphone
  6. "My account" - must be logged in to see this link. The page shows your information such as, your HOG ID number, national membership expiration date, chapter membership expiration date, chapter release expiration date and more. Hover over the "My account" link and see the following subpages;
    • "HOGWASH Newsletters" - recent, as well as past, newsletters
    • "Financial Reports" - recent, as well as past, monthly financial reports
    • "Meeting Minutes" - monthly chapter meeting minutes
    • "Chapter Merchandise" - current inventory of chapter merchandise available for sale during our monthly chapter meetings

As you can see, our website is the one stop shop for everything Raleigh HOG.

There are only a few pages that require you to be logged in to view. This is mandated by national to protect members personal information such as; last names, email addresses, phone numbers, et cetera.
