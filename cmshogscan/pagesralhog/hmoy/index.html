HOG Member Of The Year

In 2004, our dealership initiated the HOG Member of the Year award which is given each year in February to a non-officer chapter member who has demonstrated outstanding chapter, dealership, and Harley-Davidson support. Chapter members make the nomination during the general meeting the month before and the award is given to the member receiving the most nominations.
