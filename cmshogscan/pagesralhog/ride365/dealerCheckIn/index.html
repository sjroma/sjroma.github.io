Dealership Check-in Challenge

How to Participate

While you're on the road, why not make pitstops at H-D Dealers? Visit at least 10 U.S. H-D dealerships within a year to earn your first commemorative pin and patch.

Bonus: H.O.G. member with the most check-ins in a calendar year also takes home a $2,000 H-D gift card.

How it Works

  1. Ride your Harley-Davidson motorcycle to a Harley-Davidson dealership
  2. Snap a picture of yourself holding the current year Membership Guide cover with your Harley-Davidson in front of a sign displaying the dealerships name
  3. Submit your photos
  4. Win a prize! Click the link to see the official rules and prize listings

Submitting Photos for Ride 365 Challenges

Participating in H.O.G. Ride 365 Challenges like the Dealership Check-in Challenge, 10 for ’24, and 50 Rides, One Nation is simple, but members often misunderstand how to submit the required photographs to receive credit for their accomplishments. Photo submissions for these riding challenges must be uploaded to the member’s profile at members.hog.com, not through the H-D App.

Similarly, official mileage readings must be recorded at an authorized Harley-Davidson dealer to enter the Ride 365 Mileage Challenges. Miles accumulated during online riding challenges are not automatically applied to a member’s Annual or Lifetime Mileage totals.
