10 Rides for '24

None of us will get to see half of the incredible places this country has to offer in our lifetime. And with so little time to explore, you gotta go big or go home. Why waste your time on a ride that will be any less than unforgettable? We've got a whole community of riders who've seen enough of the road to help make sure we don't sleep on the good stuff. It wasn't easy, but Harley-Davidson has narrowed it down to ten trips you absolutely cannot miss. See the rules for rides for more info then get packing!

Rules for the destination challenge rides

  1. Ride your Harley-Davidson motorcycle to one of the top 10 featured locations.
  2. Snap a picture of yourself at the check-in location with a current year Membership Guide or Ride Guide cover.
  3. Submit your photo via H-D.COM/HOG.
  4. Earn digital badges and get rewarded for the ride with commemorative HOG 40th Anniversary Poker Chips and Challenge Coins.
