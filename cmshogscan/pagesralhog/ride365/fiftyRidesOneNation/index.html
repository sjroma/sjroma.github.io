50 Rides, One Nation

If you’ve got the drive to see our entire beautiful country from your bike—this one’s for you. Submit photos as you stop in each U.S. state and earn a commemorative challenge coin for each one. Visit all 50 and we’ll even install a permanent rivet with your name on it at the H-D Museum in Milwaukee, WI.

How to Participate

To participate in this challenge you will need to sign-in on Harley-Davidson's Ride 365 website and enroll in this challenge. Be sure to read the Terms & Conditions which will provide you with information on how to submit your photos. Once these steps have been completed all that is left is to get on your Harley and hit the open road.

How it Works

  1. Ride your Harley-Davidson motorcycle to one of the challenge destinations.
  2. Snap a picture of yourself holding the current year Membership Guide cover.
  3. Submit your photo.
  4. Win a digital badge and a unique souvenir for each completed ride. Complete all 50 rides and earn a large rivet that will be placed on the rivet wall at the Harley-Davidson Museum and a small rivet for the home.

Submitting Photos for Ride 365 Challenges

Participating in H.O.G. Ride 365 Challenges like the Dealership Check-in Challenge, 10 for ’24, and 50 Rides, One Nation is simple, but members often misunderstand how to submit the required photographs to receive credit for their accomplishments. Photo submissions for these riding challenges must be uploaded to the member’s profile at members.hog.com, not through the H-D App.

Similarly, official mileage readings must be recorded at an authorized Harley-Davidson dealer to enter the Ride 365 Mileage Challenges. Miles accumulated during online riding challenges are not automatically applied to a member’s Annual or Lifetime Mileage totals.
