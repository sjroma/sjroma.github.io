Raleigh HOG North Carolina 100 Counties Patch Guidelines

  • Each participant must register prior to logging visits to any counties
  • Each participant is required to maintain a log of each county visited
    • A log and counties map is included with this document
  • Log should be signed and turned in with documented visits to all 100 North Carolina counties
  • Visits to a county can be documented in either of two ways;
    1. A fellow Raleigh HOG member, who also visited the county on an H-D motorcycle with the participant, may act as a witness and sign the log for the county visited
    2. Participant may provide a photo. The photo must include the participant, any unique county landmark and the participant's Harley-Davidson motorcycle
  • Ride leaders are encouraged to provide ride participants with a list of counties included in a ride
