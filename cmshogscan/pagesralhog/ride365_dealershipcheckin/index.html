Dealership Check-in Challenge

How to Participate

To participate in this challenge you will need to sign-in on Harley-Davidson's Ride 365 website and enroll in this challenge. Be sure to read the Terms & Conditions which will provide you with information on how to submit your photos. Once these steps have been completed all that is left is to get on your Harley and hit the open road.

How it Works

  1. Ride your Harley-Davidson motorcycle to a Harley-Davidson dealership
  2. Snap a picture of yourself holding the current year Membership Guide cover with your Harley-Davidson in front of a sign displaying the dealerships name
  3. Submit your photos
  4. Win a prize! Click the link to see the contest rules for prize listings
