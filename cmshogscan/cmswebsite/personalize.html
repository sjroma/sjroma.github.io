Personalize Your Chapter's Website

Using Tokens

You can use tokens to minimize the amount to redundant typing you have to do and minimize the risk of typos.

The following table shows the token you type in on the page you are editing and the result that is displayed after you Save the page. Note; the display will show your unique chapter information. Raleigh's info is shown for display purposes only.

Token
Displays
For example...
[hogscan:chapter_name]
Chapter name
Raleigh NC Chapter
#[hogscan:chapter_number]
Chapter number
#4356
[hogscan:chapter_city]
Chapter city
Raleigh
[hogscan:chapter_state]
Chapter state
NC
[hogscan:dealer_name]
Dealer name
Tobacco Road Harley-Davidson
[hogscan:dealer_website]
Dealer website
https://tobaccoroadhd.com
[hogscan:dealer_phone]
Dealer phone
(919) 832-2261
[hogscan:dealer_address]
Dealer address
1126 S Saunders St Raleigh, NC 27603 US

About Us Page

By default, your About Us page shows a patch with a generic rocker. To make the rocker display your chapter information you will need to edit the page. The following steps will guide you.

  1. Login to your website
  2. Referencing the following screenshot you can see by default the rocker shows generic information. To change it to reflect your chapter, click the Edit tab.
    1. Referencing the screenshot below, click the down caret to expand the selection and click HTML code to select it.
    2. Referencing the screenshot below, locate the 9999 in the image (img) tag. Changing only the 9999 to your four digit chapter number. For most, it will be a four digit number. There are a few that will use a six digit number.
    3. The screenshot below shows how I edited the line to use my chapter number
    4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Save button.
  3. Your completed page displays showing the message "Basic page About Us has been updated" and the rocker shows your chapter.
