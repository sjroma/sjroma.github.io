Uniquely Raleigh HOG Patches

Raleigh HOG offers some great riding opportunities with our weekly group chapter rides. We also encourage our members to take off on their own and enjoy the great North Carolina highways and byways on their Harley-Davidson motorcycle. And why not earn a patch while doing both!

North Carolina 100 Patch

Raleigh HOG created this patch to encourage chapter members to visit all 100 North Carolina counties while riding their Harley-Davidson motorcycle. Each participating member must maintain and present a completed log to be awarded the patch. Couples should maintain a separate log for both participants. Obtain a copy of the patch rules, a log for recording county visits and a map to get started. Use the map to visually see what counties have been visited and to plan routes for counties to be visited. Credit cannot be claimed for county visits prior to officially starting.

North Carolina Five Points Patch

Visit the five cities which are considered the geographic North, South, East, West and center of North Carolina. The cities are Mount Airy (North), Calabash (South), Buxton (East), Murphy (West) and Star (center). A location has been chosen in each city where a picture can be taken with your motorcycle. Download the locations for each city, make memories and earn a patch.

Ride Into The Wild Patch

Here's a patch to bring out your wild side if you enjoy visiting zoos, animal sanctuaries and nature centers. We have compiled a list of 14 destinations to visit to earn this patch. Start your motorcycle safari and remember, please don't feed the animals.

CHDDA Patch

Do you enjoy visiting Harley-Davidson dealerships? To earn this patch, riders must visit each member dealership of the Carolina Harley-Davidson Dealers Association (CHDDA). Currently there are about 28 dealerships located in North & South Carolina with one in Virginia and one in Georgia. There is no time limit for ride completion. Purchase a poker chip or take a picture in front of the dealership. When inside the dealership, make sure you tell them you are from Tobacco Road Harley-Davidson. Follow the current member list of CHDDA dealers to complete your tour.

Eat Some Hog Patch

Ride to at least 12 different BBQ restaurants and take a photo of your motorcycle and the restaurant sign. The word Barbeque or Smoke must appear on the restaurant sign. Smithfield BBQ may only be counted once.

Chow Hound Patch

We have put together a list of 22 eating establishments gathered from the "Tar Heel Traveler" and other sources for the best food in a 100 mile radius. To earn this patch you must visit 10 different establishments. It will be interesting to hear the different opinions for each establishment. Ride to some of the chow hound locations in each city, loosen your belt and get your chow on.

Weenie Ride Patch

Do you like hotdogs? Then mount up for the "Weenie Ride" patch. There are many famous hot dog establishments across the great state of North Carolina. Ride to at least 12 different locations and take a photo of your motorcycle and the restaurant sign. (Those restaurants with multiple locations count as one. For example, you can only count one “Snoopy’s”).

NC Revolutionary War Battlefields Patch

Are you a history buff? This addition to the patch rides is the Revolutionary War Battlefields patch. There are seven stops to earn this patch. Visit each one and take a photo of your motorcycle with a battlefield sign. Get the list of NC Revolutionary War battlefields, mount your iron horse and visit history.

NC Civil War Battlefields Patch

There are 19 Civil War Battlefields in North Carolina. Visit each one and take a photo of your motorcycle with the battlefield sign. Download the list of NC Civil War battlefields, mount up and visit history.
