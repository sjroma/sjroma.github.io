Uniquely Raleigh HOG Patches

Raleigh HOG offers some great riding opportunities with our weekly group chapter rides. We also encourage our members to take off on their own and enjoy the great North Carolina highways and byways on their Harley-Davidson motorcycle. And why not earn a patch while doing both!

North Carolina 100 Patch

Raleigh H.O.G. created this patch to encourage chapter members to visit all 100 North Carolina counties while riding their Harley-Davidson motorcycle. Credit cannot be claimed for county visits prior to officially starting. Each member participating must maintain and present a completed log for a patch to be awarded to that member. Couples should maintain a separate log for both participants. Obtain a copy of the patch rules, a log for recording county visits and a map to get started. Use the map to visually see what counties have been visited and to plan routes for counties to be visited.

Eat some HOG

Ride to at least 12 different BBQ restaurants and take a photo of your motorcycle and the restaurant sign. The word Barbeque or Smoke must appear on the restaurant sign. Smithfield BBQ may only be counted once.

Civil War Battlefields Patch

There are 19 Civil War Battlefields in North Carolina. Visit all of them and take a photo of your motorcycle with the battlefield sign. Download the list of the NC Civil War battlefields, mount up and visit history.

NC Five Points Patch

Visit the five cities which are considered the geographic North, South, East, West and center of North Carolina. The cities are Mount Airy (North), Calabash (South), Buxton (East), Murphy (West) and Star (center). A location has been chosen in each city where a picture can be taken with your motorcycle. Download the locations for each city, make some memories and earn a patch.

Chow Hound Patch

We have put together a list of 22 eating establishments gathered from the "Tar Heel Traveler" and other sources for the best food in a 100 mile radius. To earn this patch you must visit 10 different establishments. It will be interesting to hear the different opinions for each establishment. Here are the chow hound locations in each city. Loosen your belt and get your chow on.

Revolutionary War Battlefields Patch

Are you a history buff? This edition to the patch rides is the Revolutionary War Battlefields patch. There are seven stops to earn this patch. Visit each one and take a photo of your motorcycle with a battlefield sign. Get the list of Revolutionary War battlefields, get on your iron horse and visit history.

Ride Into the Wild Patch

Here's a patch to bring out your wild side if you like visiting zoos, animal sanctuaries and nature centers. We have a a list of 14 destinations to visit to earn this patch. Here is your list of zoos, sanctuaries and nature centers. Remember, please don't feed the animals.

Weenie Patch

Do you like hotdogs? Then mount up for the "Weenie Ride" patch. There are many famous hot dog establishments across the great state of North Carolina. Ride to at least 12 different locations and take a photo of your motorcycle and the restaurant sign. (Those restaurants with multiple locations count as one. For example, you can only count one “Snoopy’s”).

CHDDA Patch

Do you like visiting Harley-Davidson dealerships? Then earn a patch. Visit all the Harley-Davidson dealers that are members of the Carolina Harley-Davidson Dealers Association (CHDDA). To earn the patch, riders must visit all H-D dealers that are members of CHDDA. Currently there are about 28 dealerships located in North & South Carolina with one in Virginia and one in Georgia. There is no time limit for ride completion. Purchase a poker chip or take a picture in front of the dealership. When inside the dealership, make sure you tell them you are from Tobacco Road Harley-Davidson. You will need the current member list of CHDDA dealers to complete your tour.
